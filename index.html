App Description:

Planet.com is an app that allows its users to gain access to frequently updated map images. They can navigate the map to find locations or search for specific locations in the search bar. Since the map is constantly being updated, the user may take screenshots to save past images of locations. They can then access their screenshots in a photo gallery for future analysis. Planet.com can aid in all kinds of research due to its frequently updating map. Users will first have to create an account which will be validated through email confirmation. Once this is completed, users will have access to everything they need in real time!
